Design Thinking Process

Understanding My Audience

I categorized my website visitors into recruiters, potential collaborators, teachers/mentors, and friends/family, creating personas to address their distinct needs—whether seeking my skills, projects, learning progress, or personal updates.

Identifying Key Requirements

By understanding my audience's needs, I focused my website on clear personal branding, showcasing my skills and projects, providing contact options, and ensuring responsive design for all devices.

Exploring Design Solutions

After chating with my classmates,I chose a single-page design, anchor navigation, progressive information disclosure, strong visual hierarchy, and a simple and effective way to present my best works.

Validating with Users

I showed the finished website to my family. My grandma pointed out that the font was too small, so I enhanced the contrast between the characters and simplified contact form based on user frustration
